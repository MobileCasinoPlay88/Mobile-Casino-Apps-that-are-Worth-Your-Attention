Using reality checks

Reality checks are your personal gaming assistant, helping you stay aware of your playing patterns. These automated notifications can:

  • Alert you about time spent gaming
  • Display your current session statistics
  • Provide win/loss summaries
  • Offer options to continue or take a break

Pro tip: Set reality checks at 30-60 minute intervals for optimal awareness of your gaming sessions. These reminders help you maintain control and make informed decisions about your play time.

Recognizing signs of problem gambling

Being aware of potential warning signs is crucial for maintaining a healthy relationship with mobile casino gaming. Watch out for these indicators:

  • Gambling with money meant for essential expenses
  • Chasing losses with larger bets
  • Feeling restless when trying to reduce gambling
  • Lying about gambling activities
  • Borrowing money for gambling
  • Gambling to escape problems or negative emotions

Remember: These signs don’t define you, but recognizing them early can help you maintain control and seek support if needed.
